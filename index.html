    EFFORTLESS BOOKINGS EMPOWER
    CAR WASHES, BRIGHTER RESULTS




    The purpose of this project is to revolutionize the car wash industry by developing WashWise, it
    is a third-party mobile application that helps in scheduling appointments and interacting with
    consumers more effectively. The present modes of booking like walk-ins, phone calls, and
    manual scheduling often lead to inefficiencies like long waiting periods, poor communications,
    also double and single bookings.
